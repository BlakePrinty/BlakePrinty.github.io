About me

I got into software development from playing video games and modifying little bits of code. I liked it so much I decided to take some coding classes in high school and that is when I knew that I wanted to become a software developer.

I have lived in Connecticut my whole life and grew up playing sports like lacrosse, football, soccer and basketball. After graduating high school, I decided to study Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. I then enrolled in WeCanCodeIT's full-time bootcamp specializing in Java.
